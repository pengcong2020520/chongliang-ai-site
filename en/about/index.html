About Chongliang AI

Chongliang AI is a knowledge base for beginners entering AI Product Management. It organizes core AI concepts, practical articles, and projects into a structured system for long-term learning.

Why this site exists

AI-related content grows quickly, but the valuable part is rarely a single update. It is usually a reusable judgement framework. This site organizes articles, projects, and concepts into stable entry points.

How it will evolve

The first stage establishes the static HTML skeleton and bilingual pages. The second stage imports Markdown articles and GitHub projects. The third stage expands concept pages, internal links, structured data, and GEO optimization.
